Our story

Family-Owned Auto Care Since 1989

Our mission is to provide honest, high-quality auto electrical and mechanical repair services to the Dallas community. We treat every customer like family and every vehicle like our own.

Chacho's Auto Electric was founded in 1989 by Jesus 'Chacho' Hernandez, a master technician with a passion for electrical systems. Starting in a small garage in Oak Cliff, Chacho built a reputation for solving the toughest electrical problems that other shops couldn't fix. Word spread quickly, and soon the shop expanded to serve a loyal customer base across Dallas.

Today, Chacho's sons run the business, carrying on the tradition of integrity, skill, and personal service. We've invested in the latest diagnostic technology and ongoing training for our team, but our core values remain the same: fair pricing, transparent communication, and work we're proud to stand behind.
