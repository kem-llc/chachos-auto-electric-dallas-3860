Our story

Family-Owned Auto Care Since 1985

To provide honest, high-quality auto electrical and mechanical repair to the Dallas community with integrity and transparency.

Chacho's Auto Electric was founded in 1985 by Jesus 'Chacho' Hernandez, a master electrician with a passion for solving complex automotive electrical problems. Starting as a small shop in Oak Cliff, Chacho built a reputation for diagnosing issues that other shops couldn't fix. Over the decades, the business expanded to include full mechanical services while maintaining its core focus on electrical expertise.

Today, Chacho's is run by the second generation, who uphold the same values of honesty, quality, and customer care. Our team of ASE-certified technicians serves Dallas drivers with the same dedication that made Chacho's a trusted name. We treat every vehicle as if it were our own, ensuring you leave with a safe, reliable car.
