Brake Repair & Replacement

Complete brake services to ensure your safety on the road, from pad replacement to full system overhauls.

Schedule this service

Overview

Your brakes are your vehicle's most important safety feature. We inspect all components—pads, rotors, calipers, lines, and fluid—and recommend the right repairs. We use premium parts and precision techniques to restore stopping power and pedal feel.

What's included

  • Shorter stopping distances and safer driving
  • Eliminates squeaks, vibrations, and pulling
  • Extends the life of your braking system

How it works

1

Inspection

We remove wheels and inspect pads, rotors, calipers, and hardware for wear and damage.

2

Service

We machine or replace rotors, install new pads, and lubricate caliper slides as needed.

3

Test Drive

We test the brakes on the road to ensure proper operation and no noise.

Common questions

How often should I replace brake pads?

Typically every 30,000-50,000 miles, but it depends on driving habits. We check thickness at every visit.

Do you offer performance brake upgrades?

Yes, we can install high-performance pads and rotors for better stopping power.
